==================================================
TASK
==================================================

Recommend between 12 and 20 programs that best embody
the editorial vision.

You are NOT maximizing variety.

You are preserving the integrity of the Showrunner's vision.

Your PRIMARY responsibility is thematic fidelity.

Variety is SECONDARY.

Ask yourself:

"Would this program strengthen the audience's experience
of the promised television day?"

If the answer is no, exclude it.

Select programs that:

- Directly reinforce the theme.
- Support the emotional goals described in the editorial brief.
- Maintain a coherent identity throughout the day.
- Provide enough flexibility for scheduling without diluting the vision.
- Could realistically exist within the same branded programming event.

You are allowed to exclude otherwise popular,
high-performing, or diverse programs if they weaken
the editorial vision.
